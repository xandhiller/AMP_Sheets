\textbf{Cauchy-Riemann Equations to test for Differentiability} \\ For a complex function defined as:
$$
f(z) = u(x,y) + j v(x,y)
$$

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} 
\quad \text{ and } \quad
\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = - \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}
$$ \\
